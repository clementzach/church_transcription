How to Use — Listener

Connecting

  • Enter the Session ID shared by the broadcaster — it's a 6-character code shown on their screen.
  • Select your preferred language: Spanish, Haitian Creole, Portuguese, Mandarin, or French.
  • Tap Connect. The session must already be live (broadcaster has clicked Start).

Captions

  • Translated captions appear as the speaker talks. The most recent line is shown largest; older lines fade and shrink above it.
  • Captions update in real time — there is a small delay of a few seconds while the speech is transcribed and translated.

Audio

  • Tap ▶ Start Audio in the header to enable translated text-to-speech audio playback. This button is required by browsers before audio can play.
  • The green dot next to "Audio" pulses while audio is playing.
  • If audio falls behind the live speaker, the oldest queued clip is automatically dropped to keep you in sync.

Session end

  • Sessions expire after 2 hours. When the broadcaster ends the session or it expires, you will be disconnected.
